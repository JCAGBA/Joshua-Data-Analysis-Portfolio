Project Overview

This project involved analyzing customer sales data to uncover trends and actionable insights. It highlights patterns in customer purchasing behavior and key areas for improvement.

Key Features

  • Data cleaning and preprocessing of large sales datasets
  • Interactive dashboards showcasing sales trends and performance metrics

Technologies Used

Excel, Power BI

Insights and Outcomes

The first dashboard provides insights into customer feedback and overall satisfaction. Also highlighting key areas for improvement such as customer experience with purchase and delivery and logistics, which are the most frequently mentioned themes that matter most to customers. The second dashboard shows customer feedback distribution across regions and regional sales performance. It also provides insights into the performance of sales representatives, specific customer segments, and product categories. Overall, these analyses offer insights to improve customer experience, modify sales strategies, and improve product offers based on customer sentiment and feedback..
